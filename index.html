Simon Henriksson is the main protagonist of Cry of Fear. A 19-year-old struggling with depression and trauma, he finds himself trapped in a nightmarish version of his city after a tragic accident. As he fights terrifying creatures and uncovers disturbing truths, Simon’s journey blurs the line between reality and hallucination, making him one of the most psychologically complex characters in horror gaming.
